Where x wear

 

UI Design

March 2018 | 1 week | side project | Sketch/Principle

Where x Wear is a weather forecast app with wearing recommendations. Connecting with Airbnb or Google Flights account, people could easily access their travel plan and check the weather condition. Using Instagram posts as the database, it provides hundreds of wearing recommendations based on people’s preference and their travel location. It is your great travel assistant.

 
 

On Instagram, a huge amount of people post their selfie and street photos every day, which is the output of Instagram. Where x Wear aims to develop a partnership with Instagram.

Partnership with Airbnb provides Where x Wear users quick access to their travel plan. In return, it also improves the user experience for Airbnb: display local street style and dress code, so tourists could live the local experience, which is complemented to Airbnb’s concept: enjoy the local life.
