Interstellar

 

Interstellar

October 2017 | 2 weeks | Anran Zhou & Vivian Wong

Interstellar provides a creative solution for couples who are facing long-distance relationship issue. It consists of 2 parts: a wearable bracelet to be worn and gathering heartbeat data, and a real-time interactive animation generated based on the heartbeat data. We hope that this product can help reduce the separation in LDR couples: feel your loved one’s accompanies by simply looking at your desktop.
