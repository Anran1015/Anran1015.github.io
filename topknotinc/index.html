Font

Both Proxima Nova and Open Sans are standard and widely-used font for web and mobile device. I like that modern and trendy look, with sharp and clean edge. They all have a little bit circular forms which make them look pretty “friendly”, so customers will not feel too “aloof” and “hurt” compared to other font that has sharp edge. 

Both of them have a great range of weight and style, so it is really versatile to work with any other font and design components.

Later on, we choose to use Open Sans family as the main font, as it is provided by the shopify platform. I choose to use Semibold italic as headline font to convey a sense of motion and dynamite.
