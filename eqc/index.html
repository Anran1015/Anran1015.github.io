Design Exercise

EQ Care Virtual Clinic offers Primary Care and Mental Health services to its members, yet due to the pandemic more people are seeking other wellbeing services through virtual platforms. As a UX/UI designer working for EQ you were asked to flesh out a new nutrition feature to be added on to the platform. Using your design process, tackle this feature from ideation to completion.

Requirements
1. Follow design process from ideation to completion.
2. Demonstrate your UX and UI decision guidelines.
3. Design outcomes in 2 to 3 high fidelity screens mockup.

Design Process

Research

a. User interviews: understand their habits/pain points

Participants: current EQ care end users. e.g. Bell employee

  1. Describe your typical day.

  2. Rate in the scale of 1-5, care about nutrition/Lifestyle (body builder/coach potato)?

  3. When was the last time you check up the nutrition insight/data? How often?

  4. What is your motivation/holding you back ?

  5. Currently using any nutrition apps? What do you like about it?

  6. If we are building a product…what feature would you like to see?

b. Product analysis: analog products or app with nutrition features
