Down the Rabbit Hole - VR Storytelling,

2018. Nov-Dec

Down the Rabbit Hole is an experiential VR piece inspired by Alice in Wonderland that allows the viewer to explore objects of childhood through touch and grab while floating in a mysterious tunnel. The deeper you are in the tunnel, the younger you become. Our team felt that simulating Alice’s falling journey would be an interesting use for VR, and could pose as a unique symbol and mechanic for evoking nostalgia. 

My role in this project is 3D assets creator and animator. I created and modified 3d models in C4D/Maya, and animate the object in Unity using VRTK. I created effects such as flicking books, opening doors, and record player triggering background music.

I can always find the fastest path to begin execution and have strong ability to create whatever assets needed quickly as I am fluency with industry-standard tools, and I am also resourceful in finding the best tutorials.
