Assemblr

 

AR App for Furniture Assembly

October 2017 | 2 weeks, revisit in 2019 | Sketch/PS/AE

Assemblr is a furniture assembly instruction app integrated with augmented reality and objects recognition technology. 3d models of the furniture components and assembly instruction animation showing on the screen from the user’s point of view which makes the furniture assembly process easier and more enjoyable.

 
 

Research

 

Furniture assembly can be a time-consuming task, especially for people who are not a fan of DIY. Ready-to-assemble furniture are more affordable but also takes users more time and effort to complete assembly. When the instruction is not clear, the whole experience can be even worse.
